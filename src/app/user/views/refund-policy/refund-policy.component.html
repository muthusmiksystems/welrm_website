Refund Policy

For a refund, simply contact us through the WELRM Help Center.

WELRM's Return Explanation provides flexibility and advice for planning your trip. Your confirmation email will outline the exact cancellation deadline to avoid incurring such fees. In the case of cancellation requests submitted before this deadline, you can expect a full refund of accommodation costs within 10 days of receiving the request. We do not charge a processing fee for cancellations.

However, there are exceptions to this section such as in case of unforeseen circumstances such as cancellation of a booking due to illness or injury, WELRM will provide a 100% refund.

You will get 100% only if canceled 48 hours before booking but otherwise processing free ie 2% will be deducted from the refund amount.
